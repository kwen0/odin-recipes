Cinnamon Rolls

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make a cinnamon roll.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Dough
  • Filling
  • Cream cheese frostin

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Whisk to make dough.
  3. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes.
  4. Top cinnamon rolls with cream cheese frosting.
